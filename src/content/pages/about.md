## About Me

I am Tsering Dolma, a Tibetan artist and thangka painter based in Dharamsala, Himachal Pradesh, India. I was born in Lhasa, Tibet, and came to India as a child with my family in 1994. My formal training began at the [Norbulingka Institute](https://norbulingka.org) in Dharamsala, where I studied under Kelsang Wangdu in the Menri tradition of Tibetan painting.

My work moves in two parallel streams: traditional thangka — the centuries-old form of Tibetan Buddhist devotional painting — and contemporary paintings that use the vocabulary of thangka to address subjects drawn from lived experience: exile, memory, landscape, the particular quality of life in a diaspora community.

## Training

- Trained in the **Menri tradition** of thangka painting at the Norbulingka Institute, Dharamsala (1999–2004)
- Studied under master painter **Kelsang Wangdu**
- Research residency in Gyantse, Tibet (2011)
- Residency at the Kinnaur Valley Arts Foundation (2024)
